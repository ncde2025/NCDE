Themes
of the
Conference.

Theme 1: Conceptual and Theoretical Framework of the Digital Economy.

• Definitions and concepts of the digital economy.

• Characteristics and components of the digital economy.

• The digital economy and sustainable development.

Theme 2: The Reality of the Digital Economy in Developing and Developed Countries.

• Leading international experiences in digital transformation.

• Digital readiness indicators.

• Structural challenges in adopting the digital economy.

Theme 3: Challenges Related to Digital Transformation.

• Digital infrastructure challenges.

• Cybersecurity and data protection.

• Legal and regulatory frameworks.

• Human capital and capacity building.

Theme 4: The Digital Economy and Sectoral Transformations.

• E-commerce and digital services.

• Innovation and digital entrepreneurship.

• Digitalization in the financial and banking sector.

• The digital economy in the context of the Fourth Industrial Revolution.

Theme 5: Future Prospects and Directions.

• Policies and strategies to promote the digital economy.

• Digital transformation as a driver of economic growth.

• Digitalization in the financial and banking sector.

The First National Conference on Digital Economy 2025.
