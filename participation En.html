Participation.

Participation conditions:




• The first page of the paper must include the full title of the paper, the name(s) of the author(s), academic rank, affiliated institution, and email address in both Arabic and English.



• The paper must not exceed 15 pages and must not be less than 8 pages, including the list of references, figures, and tables, and must be submitted in a Microsoft Word file.



• The APA style must be used for writing, referencing, and citations.



• The number of authors must not exceed two (02) participants.



• The plagiarism rate will be checked and must not exceed 10%.



• Authors will be notified immediately upon receipt of their final papers via an automatic message and will be informed about the review process and the need to make any required modifications.



• Deadlines must be strictly respected.



• The paper must adhere to the conference theme and relate to one of its main topics.



• The conference proceedings will be published in a conference book with an international ISBN.



• The Final paper must be sent, after the abstract is accepted, to the following email address: digitaleco2024@univ-mascara.dz.



• The paper template must be respected, Download the Paper Template.
